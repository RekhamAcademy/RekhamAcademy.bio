Rekham Academy - Geography Quiz

1. Depths of ocean water is indicated by

A. Isotherms

B. Isohyets

C. Isobars

D. Isobaths

Answer: D. Isobaths

2. During an earthquake, the point where the energy is released is called?

A. Epicentre

B. Focus or Hypocentre

C. Mantle

D. Seismic zone

Answer: B. Focus or Hypocentre

3. In which continent is the Atacama Desert located?

A. Africa

B. Asia

C. South America

D. Australia

Answer: C. South America

4. ‘Marble’, ‘Slate’, ‘Schist’ and ‘Diamond’ are examples of

A. Sedimentary rocks

B. Metamorphic rocks

C. Igneous rocks

D. Volcanic rocks

Answer: B. Metamorphic rocks

5. ‘Limestone’, ‘Sandstone’, ‘Shale’ and ‘Coal’ are examples of

A. Metamorphic rocks

B. Igneous rocks

C. Volcanic rocks

D. Sedimentary rocks

Answer: D. Sedimentary rocks

6. Torres Strait is located between?

A. New Zealand and Australia

B. Indonesia and Papua

C. Australia and New Guinea

D. Fiji and Vanuatu

Answer: C. Australia and New Guinea

7. Which city of Turkey is called ‘Gateway to the West’?

A. Ankara

B. Izmir

C. Istanbul

D. Antalya

Answer: C. Istanbul

8. Mount Tiyi is located in

A. Sikkim

B. Nagaland

C. Arunachal Pradesh

D. Mizoram

Answer: B. Nagaland

9. The coastal region where the Deltas of Godavari and Krishna rivers meet is called

A. Pulicat Lake

B. Kolleru Lake

C. Chilika Lake

D. Vembanad Lake

Answer: B. Kolleru Lake

10. ‘Nokrek’ is the highest peak of?

A. Khasi Hills

B. Mikir Hills

C. Garo Hills

D. Jaintia Hills

Answer: C. Garo Hills
